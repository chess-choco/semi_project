Chess Openings

Opening Name Description
Italian Game The Italian Game is a popular and aggressive opening for White that can lead to a quick attack on Black's kingside.
Sicilian Defense The Sicilian Defense is one of the most popular and aggressive defenses for Black. It allows Black to counterattack on the queenside while White attacks on the kingside.
French Defense The French Defense is a solid and defensive opening for Black that aims to control the center of the board.
Caro-Kann Defense The Caro-Kann Defense is a solid and defensive opening for Black that also aims to control the center of the board.
